About Me

Hello there -- my name is Dylan Fox & I hail from the great city Kansas City, MO. I have a passion for serving others, and I find my success in the success of other individuals. I am fulfilled when I utilize my technological skills to solve real-world, impactful problems. You can also catch me fulfilled when I'm around soccer, playing disc golf, running a scenic trail, or enjoying the snow-capped mountains.

I'm currently a senior at Graceland University studying Computer Science and Information Technology, Mathematics, and Data Science. Throuhgout my spell at GU, I've been grateful to serve in many distinct roles. These range from Residence Life Student Worker to Lab Monitor to Student Government Officer. These positions yield vastly different experiences -- leading me to a wide array of future opportunities and endeavors. As I look to post-graduation life, I am unsure of what the future holds. Despite the uncertainity of this, there is excitement and curiousity abound. No matter where I end up, you can be sure to see me leading the charge to lift the others around me to success, developing meaningful relationships, and using my technological skills to solve complex problems -- all in a positive light.
